We use the most current techniques to straighten misaligned teeth. Invisalign® is a ground-breaking aligner that enables discrete smile correction and so far has been responsible for straightening over 4 million smiles.

After a detailed consultation with our dentist, we custom-make your aligners. The Invisalign brace uses a series of removable aligners to gradually and gently move your teeth into the desired position. Better still, the aligners are transparent, so they’re really discreet and inconspicuous – even up close.
Each aligner should be worn for two weeks before you move onto the next one in the series: gradually moving your teeth until they reach their desired position. Along the way, we’ll keep a close eye on your progress to make sure everything is moving correctly and in the right direction.
Talk to us today and let us help you achieve the perfect confidence-boosting smile.
