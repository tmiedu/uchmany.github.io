Dermal Fillers

“Dermal fillers”are one of the most common aesthetic procedures available and are an excellent way to rejuvenate your skin, smooth wrinkles and fill out deep lines resulting in a more youthful look.

Learn More

Our body loses volume as we age and this can be very apparent in the face. Fillers can be used to return the volume and shape to cheeks, nose-to-mouth lines, mouth-to-chin (“Marionette”) lines, smoker’s lines around the mouth and they can even be used to reverse the signs of ageing in the hands.

Fillers are made of components that the body produces naturally but there are different fillers for different purposes and you will be able to discuss your tailor-made treatment plan in detail with our medical team before proceeding. We offer a selection of fillers but ensure we use the best products to give our clients the best possible results. See Prices
