Who We Are

Our aim at Esthétique Boutique is to help you rejuvenate your looks and to support you to be the best version of yourself. We are dedicated to providing our clients with a 5* service from the moment of enquiry and using the best products to fulfil our clients’ desires.

Specialising in non-surgical treatments, our UK-qualified and registered Doctor and Dentist offer an array of cosmetic procedures for men and women, ranging from anti-wrinkle injections and fillers to aesthetic dentistry such as super-fast teeth whitening and straightening. We will undertake a detailed consultation with you to ensure that we create a treatment plan that is unique to you and your goals.

Talk to us today and see what we can do for you.

Our Doctors

Dr. Tolani Graduated from the prestigious King’s College London Dental School in 2010 and she has carved out a successful career in General Dental Practice. Dr Tolani is highly skilled in multiple procedures and has a special interest in cosmetic dentistry and orthodontics.

Dr. Tolani is an Invisalign and Six-Month Smile provider and continues to build on her skills by regularly attending cosmetic dentistry courses so that she can provide a safe, up-to-date and bespoke service for her clients. She is also trained in advanced anti-wrinkle and dermal filler injections and her clinical skills will show you at your best when it matters most.

Dr Mike Ehima is an NHS Doctor who obtained his Medical Degree from Guy’s, King’s & St. Thomas’ Hospitals at King’s College London and has trained in Medicine and Surgery specialties within London and West Midland Hospitals.

Dr Mike is trained in advanced Botox and Dermal Filler techniques and provides clients with a safe and thorough service using the most-trusted products to achieve natural looking results.
